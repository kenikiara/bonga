# BONGA — Product Context

## What it is
BONGA is a free Windows voice-dictation app. Hold Right Ctrl, talk, release: clean punctuated text lands in whatever app you're typing in (WhatsApp Web, Gmail, Word, VS Code). Runs fully on-device with Whisper; no account, no cloud, no audio leaves the PC. "Bonga" is Sheng/Swahili for "talk" — the name is the pitch.

## Register
brand

## Users
Kenyan professionals, students, freelancers and small-business owners on ordinary Windows laptops. They type long WhatsApp messages, emails, reports and assignments daily. Many switch between English, Swahili and Sheng mid-sentence. Data bundles are expensive: "works offline" is a real selling point, not a checkbox.

## Brand & tone
Warm, direct, proudly Kenyan without being a tourist brochure. Sheng phrases used naturally (bonga = talk, bila stress = no stress), never explained to death. Confident like a matatu sign-writer, not corporate like a bank. Speak to "you", short sentences, no hype words.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS landing pages: purple gradient blobs, floating 3D shapes, "Supercharge your productivity".
- Safari/tourism Kenya clichés: acacia-tree sunsets, lion silhouettes.
- Corporate telco styling (Safaricom green everywhere).

## Strategic principles
1. The download button is the whole point. Free, no email, no signup. Everything on the page funnels there.
2. Show the actual product (the Flow Bar pill, the before/after text cleanup) rather than abstract illustrations.
3. Honest about being v1 from a small builder (Ken Designers, kendesigners.com). Feedback invited.
4. Later the app will be sold; the page should feel like a real product launch, not a hobby page.
